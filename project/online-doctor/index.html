LloydsPharmacy OnlineDoctor

Building an online medical record and ordering system

Sector:

Healthcare

The Challenge

To safely manage and prioritise high volumes of patients so they can be consistently and accurately diagnosed and prescribed treatment when appropriate.

The approach

The doctors workflow was analysed to identify where time is spent when reviewing, diagnosing and prescribing each patients requests for treatment.

Our bespoke system supports GPs in treating thousands of users every day
