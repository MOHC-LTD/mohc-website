John Bell & Croyden

A website redesign to match its prestigious history

Sector:

E-commerce

The goal

To rebuild their website onto a new platform that would support and further enhance John Bell & Croyden's online presence. We were invited up to their pharmacy in London and given a tour to discuss what was important to them and learn about the history of their icon location. We were then able to start developing a solution that reflected their premium brand.

The process

We explored a number of different e-commerce platforms and decided that Shopify was the best fit for this project. The team at John Bell & Croyden provided the designs and what features they would like to have and our developers made a start on bringing them to life.

An extension of their store

Take a look for yourself! A stylish, modern design built on a platform to support them into the future.

Even though the Shopify platform provides the majority of the functionality out of the box, we were able to get our hands dirty and build in some features ourselves.

  • Integration of Google Tag Manager to send event information

  • Integration of Algolia to power the websites search

  • Bespoke Shopify theme sections for content editors to shape all pages to their liking

  • Logic to calculate price per unit on items

Powerful search integration

Algolia is the power behind John Bell & Croyden's performant search functionality. We integrated the software and restyled the components to match the designs of the new website.
