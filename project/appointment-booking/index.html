Questionnaire builder

When you require information from a customer prior to an appointment, why not get them to provide it to you when they are making the booking? Create questionnaires for your customers to fill out when they book so when they arrive you can get started straight away. Our questionnaire builder can not only help you gather the information you need for a particular appointment, but it can also help guide a customer into choosing the right appointment for them. Customise the flow of the questionnaire based on how questions are answered to make sure you both get the data you need.
