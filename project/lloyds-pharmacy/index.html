We've helped LloydsPharmacy Ireland provide digital services to their customers.

Bespoke theme development

- A theme to match their branding guidelines - Timed sections for turning on/off promotional offers - Google Analytics 4 integration - Custom click and collect delivery option - Customer wish list functionality - Bespoke back in stock functionality

Online booking system

LloydsPharmacy Ireland's appointment booking service is powered by our appointment booking software. The questionnaire builder is used to capture key information to help their patients get the right treatment when they visit the chosen pharmacy.

Store locator

Finding your nearest pharmacy is easy with our Google Maps integrated store locator page that is also used to provide customers with a click and collect order option during checkout.

Search integration

Using Algolia's powerful search engine, we integrated it into LloydsPharmacy's site to help customers find what they are looking for, faster.
