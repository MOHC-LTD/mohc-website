The ability for pharmacists to approve orders for pharmacy strength medicine digitally so they can be ordered online.

Referencing the NHS dm+d database

The medicine approval system is for providing as much information to a pharmacist as possible to help them make a decision as to whether a product is suitable for a patient. It helps combine a patients order history with data from the dm+d, giving the pharmacist a clear view of the active ingredients within a patients order and how much of it they have ordered in the past.

Modern, easy to use interface

Providing the user with the information a patient has provided against the order and helping to identify any possible risks, makes reviewing orders easy.

Reporting

Each order that passes through the application, whether accepted or rejected is logged for the purpose of reporting and auditing. Easily download the data into csv or other formats, depending on your company requirements.

Approval safety nets

Using data from the NHS dm+d database, pharmacists can quickly see what ingredients exist in a customers order and determine whether it is safe for them.

Customer order history

DM+D data is also provided to a customers order history, enriching the information provided to pharmacists even more.

Since its release our app has approved thousands of pharmacy medicines and counting.
